# Patient Financial Liability Form: Investigational Botanical Protocol

**Document Type:** Private Financial Responsibility Agreement / Advanced Beneficiary Notice (ABN) Equivalent  
**File Location:** `/docs/forms/patient-financial-liability.md`  
**Protocol Reference:** IRB-Approved Compassionate Care Exception for *Trigonella foenum-graecum* (Alhalba-NB Protocol)

---

### **IMPORTANT NOTICE TO PATIENT / LEGAL GUARDIAN**
Your medical team is integrating a highly specialized, optimized, and concentrated formulation of *Trigonella foenum-graecum* (referred to as Alhalba-NB-1350mg / Alhalba-NB-3000mg) into your neurosurgical treatment plan to manage and reduce brain swelling. 

Please read this form carefully. It explains that while your standard medical care and physician consultations will be billed to your health insurance provider, the specialized botanical supplement itself is **strictly non-covered** by health insurance and requires direct, out-of-pocket payment.

---

### 1. Separation of Billed Services (The Hybrid Model)
By signing below, you acknowledge and agree to the following financial structure:
*   **Insurance-Billed Services:** The specialized cognitive evaluations, dosing management, laboratory monitoring, and advanced clinical care provided by the attending Integrative Medicine physicians and Neurosurgeons will be billed directly to your commercial health insurance plan or Medicare. Standard co-pays, deductibles, and co-insurance rules apply.
*   **Out-of-Pocket Supplement Cost:** The specialized procurement, custom compounding, and administrative costs for the proprietary *Alhalba-NB* formulations are **not** covered by any third-party health insurance payer, government program (including Medicare/Medicaid), or healthcare reimbursement account. You assume full personal financial liability for these costs.
