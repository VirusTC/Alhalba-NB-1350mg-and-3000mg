Dear Division Director,

Pursuant to Section 561(b) of the Federal Food, Drug, and Cosmetic Act and 21 CFR § 312.310, I am writing to submit an application for an Individual Patient Expanded Access IND. This request authorizes the use of a specialized, optimized botanical configuration of *Trigonella foenum-graecum* (Alhalba-NB-1350mg and Alhalba-NB-3000mg) for an individual patient who presents with life-threatening peritumoral and post-traumatic cerebral edema following a primary neurosurgical craniotomy. 

### 1. Clinical Rationale and Target Population
The patient under my care has exhausted conventional medical approaches for managing structural cerebral expansion. Continued high-dose synthetic corticosteroid therapy (Dexamethasone) has resulted in severe systemic metabolic toxicity, including steroid-induced hyperglycemia and refractory insulin resistance. 

The Alhalba-NB protocol introduces a dual-action mechanism targeting alternative biochemical pathways to manage swelling. The optimized phyto-alkaloid matrix leverages **Trigonelline** to suppress Matrix Metalloproteinase-9 (MMP-9) and downregulate astrocytic Aquaporin-4 (AQP4) expressions, directly arresting vasogenic and cytotoxic fluid leaking into the parenchyma. Simultaneously, the inclusion of **4-hydroxyisoleucine** acts as a glucose-dependent insulin secretagogue to offset synthetic corticosteroid-induced endocrine shifts. 

### 2. Treatment Location and Monitoring Infrastructure
The initial induction phase (Tier 1: Alhalba-NB-3000mg) will be administered inside the Neurovascular Intensive Care Unit (ICU) under joint neurosurgery and integrative medicine oversight. Upon achieving radiological and clinical stability, the patient will transition to an outpatient take-home maintenance phase (Tier 2/4: Alhalba-NB-1350mg) under strict regulatory compliance frameworks. 

To ensure absolute safety, the clinical protocol mandates:
*   Continuous fingerstick blood glucose logging to monitor for insulinotropic hypoglycemia.
*   Bi-weekly coagulation profile checks (PT/INR, aPTT) to monitor the botanical compound's natural coumarin profile.
*   Serial structural T2/FLAIR MRI volumetric calculations to track objective edema resolution.

### 3. Separation of Professional Services and Compound Logistics
As the Sponsor-Investigator, I maintain absolute transparency regarding institutional operations. Third-party commercial insurance and federal plans will be billed exclusively for professional cognitive labor and diagnostic procedures using time-based E/M codes (99205–99233). The proprietary compounding, chemical optimization, and procurement costs of the physical *Alhalba-NB* variants are strictly unbundled and handled via an explicit, out-of-pocket Private Financial Responsibility Agreement executed by the patient's legal guarantor.
