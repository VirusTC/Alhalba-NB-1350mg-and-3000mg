I. Nature of the Experimental Adjuvant

You are consenting to participate in a coordinated, single-patient clinical protocol utilizing oral [Nutricost Fenugreek Seed Powder](https://www.amazon.com/Nutricost-Fenugreek-Seed-1500mg-Capsules/dp/B01A1CWDC4) (1,350 mg per serving, comprised of 2 capsules). This botanical product is classified as a dietary supplement by the FDA rather than a prescription medication. It is introduced here solely as an experimental, supportive option to aid metabolic homeostasis during your concussion recovery framework. [[1](https://www.amazon.com/Nutricost-Fenugreek-Seed-1500mg-Capsules/dp/B01A1CWDC4), [2](https://www.vitacost.com/nutricost-fenugreek), [3](https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC9614271/)]

II. Explicit Acknowledgment of Risks

By signing below, you confirm that you fully understand the following medical risks:

-   **Airway Reactivity and Coughing:** Ingestion of any oral substance poses an inherent risk of throat irritation, swallowing dysfunction, or coughing. **Coughing can violently elevate your blood pressure and alter volume mechanics inside your head.** If a surgical intervention becomes necessary, coughing poses an immediate threat to life and neurological safety.
-   **Hemostasis & Bleeding Tendencies:** Fenugreek contains natural compounds that may mildly delay blood clotting. This can increase the risk of unexpected bruising, microvascular leaking, or internal hematoma formatting.
-   **Administrative Actions:** Your insurance carrier treats this intervention as experimental; any non-standard medical tracking or provider hours may not be covered by standard coverage.

III. Voluntary Participation & Right to Revoke

Your participation is entirely voluntary. The primary neurosurgical team has the legal right and medical duty to cancel this protocol at any millisecond if they believe your structural safety or blood clotting is being compromised.
